BUC Timeline (Residential)

Viewing to Booking (days): 14 days

Booking Date ~ [Pay 5% Cash Booking Fee to get OTP]

Booking to Receipt of S&P and OTP (days): 14 days

OTP and S&P Received ~ [10 - 14 days]

OTP to Exercise (days): 21 days

Exercise Date ~ [Engage Law Firm to Exercise] [within 21 days after receiving OTP and S&P]

Exercise to Stamp Duty (days): 14 days

Stamp Duty Date ~ [Pay Stamp Duty] [within 14 days after exercise]

Stamp Duty to Downpayment (days): 7 days

Downpayment Date ~ [Pay 15% Cash/CPF Booking Fee, No more than 8 weeks from Booking Date]

Days to Complete Foundation Work (days): 270 days

Foundation Completed ~ [6 - 12 months; Pay 10% of which 5% Cash/CPF]

Days to Reinforced Concrete Framework (days): 240 days

Reinforced Concrete Framework Completed ~ [6 - 9 months; Pay 10%]

Days to Partition Walls (days): 160 days

Partition Walls Completed ~ [3 - 6 months; Pay 5%]

Days to Roofing (days): 160 days

Roofing Completed ~ [3 - 6 months; Pay 5%]

Door, Window, Electrical (days): 160 days

Door, Window, Electrical Completed ~ [3 - 6 months; Pay 5%]

Carpark, Road, Drain (days): 160 days

Carpark, Road, Drain Completed ~ [3 - 6 months; Pay 5%]

Sewerage, Utilities (days): 160 days

Sewerage, Utilities Completed ~ [3 - 6 months; Pay 25%]

TOP Date ~ [usually TOP in previous step]
